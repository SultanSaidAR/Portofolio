My Profile

Hi, I’m Sultan Said Abdul Rahim, a 3rd-year Information Systems student at Tarumanagara University with a deep passion for technology. I have a particular interest in data analysis and web development, where I strive to turn data into meaningful insights and create user-friendly web solutions.With hands-on experience in HTML, CSS, and PHP, I have built functional websites and dashboards tailored to support data analytics. From structuring clean, responsive web interfaces to crafting data-driven dashboards, I enjoy combining logic with creativity to deliver impactful results. Driven by a desire to keep learning and growing, I’m excited to take my skills to the next level as a Data Analyst and Web Developer in a professional environment. I believe that every project is an opportunity to improve, innovate, and make a positive impact through technology. Let’s connect and create something great together!
